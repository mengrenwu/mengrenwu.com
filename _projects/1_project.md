- Developed a modular 5-axis CNC automation platform for imaging-assisted precision transfer, integrating mechanical design, motion control, and automated workflow generation.
- Optimized the 300 μm needle end-effector to improve stability and achieve 0.15 mm bidirectional resolution with speeds up to 45 mm/s.
- Engineered multi-process holder assemblies across Nanoscribe, SLA, and FDM components with tolerance-aware design for reliable precision operation.

The platform combines modular mechanical architecture with motion-control workflows to support repeatable transfer operations under imaging guidance. The holder assemblies were designed across multiple fabrication processes to balance precision, manufacturability, and rapid iteration.
